About Me

Get to know the person behind the data & code

I’m a passionate Data & ML Engineer and Front-End Developer who loves turning ideas into clean, interactive products. On the front end, I enjoy crafting responsive, intuitive UIs that feel fast, modern, and easy to use. On the ML side, I’m driven by how models can move from notebooks into real interfaces that people actually interact with.

I like working end-to-end — from feature engineering and model building to wiring those models into front-end experiences using APIs and clean component architecture. My focus is on writing readable, production-ready code, designing UIs that explain the ML behind them, and making sure everything feels smooth for the user, not just impressive in theory.

When I’m not experimenting with new ML ideas, you’ll usually find me refining UI details, exploring modern front-end patterns, or learning how teams ship AI features at scale. My goal is to sit at the intersection of ML, front-end engineering, and product, building systems that are technically solid and genuinely enjoyable for people to use.
